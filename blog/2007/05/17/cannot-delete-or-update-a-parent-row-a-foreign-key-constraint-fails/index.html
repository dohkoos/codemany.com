Cannot Delete or Update a Parent Row: A Foreign Key Constraint Fails

May 17th, 2007

在SQLyog中Import Data或Empty Table时，出现：

这可能是MySQL在InnoDB中设置了foreign key关联，造成无法更新或删除数据。可以通过设置FOREIGN_KEY_CHECKS变量来避免这种情况。

参考资料： Setting FOREIGN_KEY_CHECKS to 0 can also be useful for ignoring foreign key constraints during LOAD DATA and ALTER TABLE operations.
